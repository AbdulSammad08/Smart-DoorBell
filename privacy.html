Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to our mission. This policy outlines how we handle the personal information collected through our IoT devices and mobile applications. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We collect information you provide directly, such as your account details, and information generated automatically by our devices, such as system health logs.

videocam

Camera & Facial Data

All video recordings are processed with end-to-end encryption. Our facial recognition technology operates locally on the device wherever possible to minimize data transmission.

  • Edge Processing: Motion detection and basic object classification happen on-device.
  • Biometric Data: Facial templates are encrypted and stored separately from video files.
  • User Control: You can purge your video history and facial recognition database at any time through the SmartBell app.
cloud_done

Microsoft Azure Cloud Storage

We utilize Microsoft Azure enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure to provide high-availability storage for your recordings.

Why Azure?

By leveraging Azure's global data centers, we ensure your data is stored in compliance with regional regulations (GDPR, CCPA). Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. SmartBell employees do not have access to your private decryption keys.
